Welcome to the Tor network!

This is a simple site meant for me to mess around with darkhttpd and web hosting, hi

I also might as well teach you about proper OPSEC while I'm here. The short version is don't be a dumbass, turn off JavaScript as 99% of .onion websites won't need it and it can be used to deanonymize you, and I would personally recommend using a privacy-focused OS like Tails, Qubes, or Whonix.

Good OPSEC takes a little bit to learn. Go browse Dread or watch some YouTube videos, I like watching Mental Outlaw, he makes good content.

If you want to quickly get around Tor, go to tor.taxi, but I recommend you visit their onion instead.

Thank you for visiting my silly little website! Enjoy browsing Tor, and remember to stay safe out there.
